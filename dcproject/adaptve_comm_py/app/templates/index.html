How it works

Follow the path your data takes from text/file input to decoded output.

STEP 1

Encrypt & Protect

TX page AES‑GCM encrypts your text or files and wraps the ciphertext with repetition‑3 FEC for resilience.

STEP 2

Adaptive Modulation

The ML model inspects the channel SNR and switches between BPSK, QPSK, or 16QAM before sending symbols.

STEP 3

Visualize & Learn

Constellations, I/Q waveforms, and bitstreams animate in real time so you can see noise distortions.

STEP 4

Receive & Decrypt

The RX page FEC‑decodes, decrypts, and offers downloads while showing channel updates and logs.
